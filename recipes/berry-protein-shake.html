Berry Protein Shake

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Almond Milk
  • 30 grams of Protein Powder
  • 90 grams of Non-fat greek yogurt
  • 145 grams Frozen mixed berries
  • One whole banana

Steps

  1. Pour a cup of almond milk inside the blender.
  2. Peel a banana and add to the blender.
  3. Add a serving of protein powder.
  4. Add in the greek yogurt.
  5. Add in the serving of frozen mixed berries
  6. BLEND AND ENJOY!
